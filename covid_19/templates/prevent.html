Things Not To Do During Covid

The pandemic comes with a lot of restrictions for every individual citizen so as to inhibit the spread of the virus, some of these are :

  • Do Not Gather In Masses
  • Do Not Share Eating
  • Do Not Touch Your Face, Nose Or Eyes While Outside

Things To Do During Covid

If you think that you are having symptoms of coronavirus, don't panic, follow the protocols given below :

  • Wash Your Hands At Regular Intervals
  • Wear A Mask
  • Seek Medical Attention Immediately
